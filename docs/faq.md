# Teezy FAQ

---

## General

**What is Teezy?**
Teezy is a social golf booking app. Find courses based on your vibe, book tee times, compete with friends, climb a ranking system, join leagues, and share your game on a social feed.

**Is Teezy free?**
Yes — Teezy is 100% free for golfers. There are no subscriptions, no fees, and no in-app purchases. Courses pay a small per-booking fee, which keeps the platform free for players.

**Is Teezy available on iOS and Android?**
Yes. Teezy is available on both iOS and Android as well as on the web at teezy.app.

**Do I need an account to browse courses?**
You can browse courses on the discovery map without an account, but you'll need to sign up to book a tee time, join leagues, or access the social features.

## Account & Privacy

**How do I sign up?**
Tap **Sign Up** on the home screen and register with your email address or a social login (Apple, Google).

**What is the difference between a public and private account?**
- **Public:** Anyone on Teezy can see your profile, stats, and posts.
- **Private:** Only approved friends can see your stats, posts, and availability. Your username and profile photo are still visible to others.

**How do I switch between public and private?**
Go to your **Profile** → **Settings** → **Privacy** and toggle Public / Private.

**Can I control what my friends see?**
If your account is public, friends see everything by default. If it's private, only approved connections have access. You cannot currently restrict specific items for specific friends beyond the public/private toggle.

## Booking

**How do I book a tee time?**
Open the app, browse courses on the map or list view, select a course and available tee time, and book. You can invite friends to form a Tee Time Party during the booking flow.

**Can I make multiple bookings at once?**
Yes. You can have multiple upcoming bookings at the same time.

**Can I book in advance?**
Yes. You can book days or weeks ahead depending on the course's available schedule.

**How do I cancel a booking?**
Go to **My Bookings** and tap the booking you want to cancel. Tap **Cancel Booking**. Cancellation policies are set by each course individually.

## Tee Time Parties & Game Modes

**What is a Tee Time Party?**
A Tee Time Party is a group booking with up to 4 players. See [Tee Time Parties](./players/tee-time-parties.md) for full details.

**What is the difference between Chill, Fun, and Competitive?**
- **Chill** — casual, scores do not affect your rank.
- **Fun** — scores count toward rank.
- **Competitive** — scores count toward rank, weighted more heavily.

See [Game Modes](./players/game-modes.md) for full details.

**Can I change game modes after the round starts?**
No. Mode is locked once the round begins.

---

## Rankings

**How does the ranking system work?**
Your main rank is based on scores submitted in Fun or Competitive rounds, adjusted for course difficulty and frequency of play. See [Ranking System](./players/ranking-system.md) for all the details.

**What are the rank tiers?**
Rookie → Amateur → Club Player → Scratch → Elite → Tour → Legend → Unreal (top %)

**Does my rank go down?**
Yes. Consistent poor performance in Competitive rounds can lower your rank. Chill rounds have no effect.

**What is the Unreal rank?**
Unreal is a special tier for the top percentage of players — not a fixed number of spots.

**Do I have separate ranks for 1v1 and 2v2 Scramble?**
Yes. Your Main Rank, 1v1 Rank, and 2v2 Scramble Rank are all tracked independently.

## Leagues

**How do leagues work?**
Any player can create a league, invite friends, and run a season with standings and a playoff bracket. See [Leagues](./players/leagues.md) for details.

**Can I be in multiple leagues?**
Yes. There is no limit.

**Do league results affect my main rank?**
No. League results are tracked separately.

---

## Mini Tournaments

**What is a mini tournament?**
Courses set up short competitive windows where any player who books a round can opt in and compete on a leaderboard. See [Mini Tournaments](./players/mini-tournaments.md).

**Do mini tournament results affect my rank?**
No. Tournament results are separate from the main ranking system.

**Why do I need a playing partner to enter?**
To maintain score integrity — solo scores without a witness are not eligible.

---

## Availability Calendar

**What is the availability calendar?**
A weekly tool where you mark which days you're available to play. Friends can see your availability to make it easier to coordinate rounds. See [Availability Calendar](./players/availability-calendar.md).

**Is setting my availability required?**
No. It's completely optional.

## Social

**What appears in my social feed?**
Round results from friends, rank-up notifications, swing videos, course events and promotions, league match results, and mini tournament results.

**How do I add friends?**
Search for a player by username and send a friend request, or use the invite feature from within a Tee Time Party.

**Can I see my friends' bookings?**
Yes, if their account is public or you're approved friends with a private account holder.

**How does pricing work for courses?**
$2.75 per completed booking, invoiced monthly. Discounts are available down to $1.50 for founding courses. See [Pricing Model](./courses/pricing-model.md).

**What dashboards do course staff get?**
Two separate dashboards: Pro Shop (daily operations) and Manager/Owner (analytics, billing, profile, events, tournaments). See [Dashboard Guide](./courses/dashboard-guide.md).
