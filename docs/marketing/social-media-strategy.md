# PAR-Tee Social Media Strategy

## Brand Voice
**Competitive. Real. Yours.**

PAR-Tee speaks to golfers who take their game seriously — even when they're being casual about it. Every golfer has pride. We speak to that. The voice is direct, confident, and knows golf. No fluff, no hashtag spamming, no watered-down inspiration content.

**Tone guidelines:**
- Speak like you've played 1000 rounds, not like a marketing team
- Short sentences win. Golf content is mobile-first.
- Stats and specificity beat vague claims
- Humor is welcome — golf has a great culture of dry wit
- Never sound corporate or generic

**Brand tagline variations:**
- "Book Golf by Mood"
- "Competitive. Real. Yours."
- "Golf for people who give a damn"
- "Your tee time. Your leaderboard."

---

## Platforms

### Instagram (@partee.golf)
- **Focus:** Visual-first posts, course photography, round-ready content
- **Format:** Mix of static posts, Reels (short-form video), Stories
- **Audience:** Golfers 22–45 in Edmonton, Calgary, and broader Alberta
- **Frequency:** 3 posts/week minimum (Mon, Wed, Fri ideal cadence)

### TikTok (@partee.golf)
- **Focus:** Golfer identity content, golf humor, "golfer vs. golfer" competitive formats
- **Format:** Short video (15–60s), trending audio, relatable scenarios
- **Audience:** Younger golfers 18–35, newer golfers, competitive types
- **Frequency:** 3–5 posts/week minimum; TikTok rewards volume
