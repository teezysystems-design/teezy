## 1. Course Partner Agreement Summary

*This is a plain-English summary. The full legal agreement will be reviewed by legal/CTO before execution with partners.*

### What PAR-Tee Provides
- A listing on the PAR-Tee platform with real-time tee sheet integration
- Golfer-facing booking via mobile app and web
- Automatic payment processing via Stripe Connect
- Course dashboard: Pro Shop View (booking management) and Manager View (analytics)
- Access to PAR-Tee's golfer community in Edmonton and Calgary
- Founding Partner badge and placement for early partners

### What the Course Provides
- Accurate tee sheet availability and pricing (maintained by course staff)
- Commitment to honor all confirmed PAR-Tee bookings
- A valid Stripe Connect account for receiving payouts
- Permission to display course name, description, and submitted photos on the platform

### Pricing Terms
- **Standard rate:** $2.75 per confirmed booking (deducted at payout)
- **Founding Partner rate:** $1.50 per confirmed booking — permanently locked in for courses that partner before public launch
- No monthly subscription fee
- No setup fee

### Payout Terms
- Stripe processes payments at time of golfer booking
- PAR-Tee deducts the flat booking fee
- Net amount deposited to course's linked bank account within 2 business days

### Cancellations
- Course sets its own cancellation policy, displayed to golfers at booking
- On cancellations within the course's stated window, full greens fee is refunded to golfer; PAR-Tee waives the booking fee
- Course keeps any greens fee amounts per its own policy on late/no-show cancellations

### Term and Termination
- No minimum contract term
- Either party may terminate with 30 days notice
- Founding Partner rate is maintained for the duration of active partnership in good standing
- PAR-Tee reserves the right to suspend a course listing for consistent booking non-fulfillment

### Intellectual Property
- Course grants PAR-Tee a limited license to display course content (name, photos, description) on the platform for the purpose of facilitating bookings

## 5. FAQ for Course Operators

**Q: How does the tee sheet integration work?**
A: Course staff manage a live tee sheet via the PAR-Tee dashboard. Availability updates in real time — golfers only see open slots.

**Q: Do we need new hardware or software?**
A: No. The dashboard is web-based. Any computer, tablet, or phone with a browser works.

**Q: What happens if we overbook by mistake?**
A: The system prevents double-booking on confirmed PAR-Tee slots. For conflicts with your own direct bookings, your staff manages the tee sheet as they would any channel.

**Q: Can we set different pricing for PAR-Tee vs. our website?**
A: Yes. You control pricing per slot. You can price PAR-Tee bookings identically to your direct bookings or use the channel for twilight/discounted rates.

**Q: What if a golfer is a no-show?**
A: Your existing no-show policy applies. If the slot is forfeited per your policy, you keep the greens fee amount per your stated terms. PAR-Tee's booking fee applies only to confirmed bookings that were honored.

**Q: How do we get paid?**
A: Via Stripe Connect. You'll connect a bank account during onboarding. Payouts are automatic within 2 business days of each booking.
