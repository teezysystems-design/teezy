**MOOD-FIRST DISCOVERY**
Before you search, pick your mood. PAR-Tee filters available tee times at courses tagged to match. No more guessing whether River Ridge is the right vibe for today. We tell you.

**BOOK INSTANTLY**
Real-time availability. No cached tee sheets, no phantom times. If it shows, you can book it. Confirm in under 60 seconds with Apple Pay or your card.

**FLAT $2.75 BOOKING FEE**
One fee. Every booking. No percentage of your greens fee, no dynamic pricing, no surprise totals at checkout. What you see is what you pay.

**COMPETE WITH LOCAL GOLFERS**
PAR-Tee has a real leaderboard. Track your handicap, log every round, and see where you rank among golfers in your city. Issue 1v1 match play challenges or organize scrambles from right inside the app.

**TEE TIME PARTY SYSTEM**
Invite friends, claim a time slot as a group, and lock it in together. Each person pays their own share. No more "who's booking?" texts before every round.

**SOCIAL FEED**
Post your scorecard, share round highlights, and follow local golfers. A golf community built around actual play — not sponsored content.

**SCORE TRACKING & ANALYTICS**
Log hole-by-hole scores, track fairways hit and GIR, and watch your handicap trend over the season. Data that actually helps you improve.

**AI MOOD MATCHING**
Our matching engine gets smarter as you play. The more rounds you log, the better PAR-Tee understands your game and surfaces the right courses for your current skill and mood.
