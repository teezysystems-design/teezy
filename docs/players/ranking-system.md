# Ranking System

## How Ranking Works

Teezy's ranking system is designed to feel like a real competitive game — think ranked mode in Fortnite or League of Legends, but for golf. Every Fun or Competitive round you play moves your rank up or down based on your scores, your consistency, and how often you play.

### What Counts Toward Your Rank

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Courses played | More rounds = more rank opportunities |
| Scores submitted | Lower scores = better rank movement (adjusted for course difficulty) |
| Frequency of play | Playing regularly keeps your rank active |
| Round mode | Fun and Competitive rounds count; **Chill rounds do not** |

---

## Rank Tiers

There are 8 tiers. Work your way up from Rookie to the top:

| Tier | Name |
|------|------|
| 1 | Rookie |
| 2 | Amateur |
| 3 | Club Player |
| 4 | Scratch |
| 5 | Elite |
| 6 | Tour |
| 7 | Legend |
| 8 | Unreal *(top % of players)* |

Each tier has its own badge displayed prominently on your profile. When you rank up, it appears in the social feed so your friends can see your progress.

---

## Three Separate Rankings

Your profile tracks three distinct rankings:

1. **Main Rank** — based on all Fun and Competitive rounds
2. **1v1 Rank** — tracks head-to-head challenge results from 1v1 games within parties
3. **2v2 Scramble Rank** — tracks team challenge results from 2v2 scramble games

Each rank climbs independently. You could be Tour rank in main, Amateur in 1v1, and somewhere in the middle for 2v2.

## Leaderboards

- View leaderboards from the Ranking tab
- Filter by: **Main Rank**, **1v1**, **2v2 Scramble**
- Compare yourself to friends or all players globally

---

## Handicap

Your traditional golf handicap is calculated alongside your rank and displayed on your profile. It updates automatically after every Fun or Competitive round you submit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does playing more often always help my rank?**
Playing regularly keeps your rank from becoming inactive, and more rounds give you more opportunities to improve. However, consistently bad scores in Competitive mode can lower your rank.

**How is course difficulty factored in?**
Teezy adjusts scores based on each course's difficulty rating. A great score on a tough course is worth more than the same score on an easy one.

**Can my rank go down?**
Yes. In Competitive rounds especially, poor performance can lower your rank. Fun rounds are weighted more leniently.

**What is "Unreal" rank?**
Unreal is a special designation for a small percentage of top-ranked players. It is not a fixed number — it's determined by where you stand relative to the rest of the player base.

**How do I see my friends' ranks?**
Go to any friend's profile to see their current rank badge, tier, and full year-to-date stats.
