# Tee Time Parties

## What Is a Tee Time Party?

A Tee Time Party is a group booking with up to **4 players**. When you create or join a party, everyone is automatically added to the same tee time — no separate bookings needed.

## Creating a Party

1. Select a course and choose a tee time.
2. Tap **Create Party**.
3. Invite friends from your friends list. Each friend gets a notification.
4. When a friend accepts, they are automatically added to the booking.
5. Before teeing off, the party picks a **Game Mode** (see below).

## Joining a Party

When a friend invites you, you'll receive a notification. Tap **Accept** to join — you're now part of the booking. If you decline, the party host can invite someone else.

## Game Mode Selection

Before your round begins, the party votes on a mode:

| Mode | What it means |
|------|---------------|
| **Chill** | Casual round. Scores do NOT count toward your rank. |
| **Fun** | Scores count toward main ranking. Relaxed competitive vibe. |
| **Competitive** | Full competitive mode. Scores count toward main ranking. |

> **Note:** Only Fun and Competitive rounds update your rank. Chill rounds are off the record.

## In-Round Challenges

In Fun or Competitive mode, your party can activate a head-to-head challenge format:

- **1v1** — hole-by-hole scoring between two players
- **2v2 Scramble** — two-player teams, best ball format

More challenge formats are coming in future updates.

## Score Tracking

Log scores hole by hole during the round using the in-app scorecard. When you finish, submit your scores. Your ranking, league records, and any active mini tournament leaderboard will all update automatically.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I book a tee time by myself?**
Yes. You can create a party with just yourself and book a solo round. You can still invite friends later if they are available.

**What happens if a friend declines or doesn't respond?**
The party proceeds with whoever accepted. You can invite a different friend to fill the open spot.

**Can I be in multiple parties at once?**
No. You can only be in one active tee time party at a time. You can have multiple upcoming bookings, but only one pending party invitation at a time.

**Does a Chill round affect my handicap?**
No. Chill rounds are not submitted to the ranking or handicap system.
