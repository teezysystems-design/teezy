# Availability Calendar

## What Is the Availability Calendar?

The availability calendar is a simple weekly tool to help you and your friends coordinate rounds without the endless "when are you free?" texts. Set your available days for the week, and your friends can see when you're free — making it easy to organize a tee time party.

It's completely optional. You only set it if you want to.

---

## How to Set Your Availability

1. Every **Monday**, you'll get a prompt to set your available days for the upcoming week.
2. Go to the **Calendar** tab and tap the days you're available.
3. Save. Your friends who follow you (or are on your friends list) can now see your availability.

You can update your availability at any time during the week.

---

## Viewing Friends' Availability

From the **Calendar** tab, switch to a friend's view to see which days they've marked as available. If multiple friends are available on the same day, you'll see that clearly — making it easy to find a day that works for everyone.

> **Privacy note:** Availability is only visible to friends if your account is public or you've approved the friend connection. Private accounts hide availability from non-friends.

---

## Booking Directly from the Calendar

When you're viewing availability and find a day that works for your crew:

1. Tap **Create Tee Time Party** from the calendar view.
2. The app will suggest tee times at nearby courses that day.
3. Invite your friends directly from the calendar. The party is created and invitations are sent.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I have to set my availability every week?**
No. It's optional. If you don't set it, your friends simply won't see availability for you that week — they'll need to reach out directly.

**Can my availability be seen by anyone on Teezy?**
Only your friends can see your availability (or anyone if your profile is public). If your account is set to private, only approved friends see it.

**Can I mark myself available for specific times of day, not just days?**
Currently, availability is set per day (not time-specific). Time-based availability may be added in a future update.

**What if my plans change after I've marked myself available?**
Just update your calendar — tap the day again to remove it. Your friends will see the updated status.
