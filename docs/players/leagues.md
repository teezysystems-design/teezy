# Leagues

## What Is a League?

A league is a private, season-based competition you run with your friends. Any player can create a league, set the rules, invite others, and compete across a full season — ending with a playoff bracket to crown the winner.

Leagues are completely separate from your main rank. They're about bragging rights with your crew.

---

## League Types

### 1v1 League

Head-to-head match play between individual players.

- Any player creates the league and sets the season length.
- Players schedule matches between themselves throughout the season.
- Full-season standings are tracked.
- **Playoff bracket** at the end of the season to determine the champion.
- Results are tracked in your **1v1 league history** on your profile.

### 2v2 Scramble League

Team-based competition in scramble format.

- Same structure as 1v1 — one creator, set season length, scheduled matches.
- League creator sets the **partner rule**:
  - **Locked partner** — same partner for the entire season
  - **Open partner** — players can choose a different partner for each match
- Playoff bracket at season end.
- Results tracked in your **2v2 scramble league history** on your profile.

---

## How to Create a League

1. Go to the **Leagues** tab and tap **Create League**.
2. Choose the league type: 1v1 or 2v2 Scramble.
3. Set the season length (start and end date).
4. For 2v2, choose the partner rule (locked or open).
5. Invite players from your friends list.
6. Once everyone accepts, the season begins.

## How to Join a League

You'll receive an invite notification when someone adds you. Tap **Accept** to join. Once enough players have accepted, the season starts automatically on the set start date.

---

## During the Season

- **Schedule matches** directly with your opponent — pick a course and tee time that works for both of you.
- Play the match as a regular Tee Time Party in Fun or Competitive mode.
- The result is submitted to the league standings automatically.
- Use the **league chat** to trash talk, coordinate, and celebrate.

## League Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| League chat | Group chat for all league members |
| Season standings | Live standings table throughout the season |
| Match history | Full record of every match played |
| Playoff bracket | Visual bracket at season end |
| Winner badge | League champion badge displayed on your profile |

---

## Playoffs

When the regular season ends, the top players (or teams) enter the playoff bracket. The bracket is single-elimination. Results are posted in the league and in the social feed.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I be in multiple leagues at the same time?**
Yes. You can participate in multiple leagues simultaneously.

**Is there a limit to how many players can join a league?**
There is no hard cap, but leagues work best with 4–16 players for a clean bracket structure.

**Do league matches count toward my main rank?**
No. League results are tracked separately and do not affect your main rank. You still need to play in Fun or Competitive mode for the round to affect rank.

**What if a match doesn't get played before the season ends?**
The league creator can set match deadlines. If a match isn't played, the league admin can record a forfeit or decide to extend the deadline.

**Can I leave a league mid-season?**
You can leave, but your match results remain on the record. Contact the league creator to discuss how unplayed matches will be handled.
