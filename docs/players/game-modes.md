# Game Modes

## Overview

Every Tee Time Party picks a game mode before the round starts. Your choice determines whether scores are tracked, how they count toward your rank, and which in-round challenges are available.

---

## The Three Modes

### Chill

A casual, no-pressure round.

- Scores are **not submitted** to the ranking system.
- No in-round challenges available.
- Great for practicing, playing with new golfers, or just enjoying the day.
- Still tracked in your round history so you can look back at your personal play.

### Fun

Competitive, but relaxed.

- Scores **count toward your main rank**.
- In-round challenges (1v1, 2v2 Scramble) are available.
- Scores also update your **handicap**.
- Perfect for regular rounds with friends where you want the results to mean something.

### Competitive

Full send.

- Scores **count toward your main rank**, weighted more heavily than Fun rounds.
- In-round challenges are available.
- Scores update your **handicap**.
- Use this when you're grinding your rank and playing your best golf.

---

## In-Round Challenge Formats

When playing Fun or Competitive, activate a challenge to add a structured competition within your party:

### 1v1

- Two players compete head to head, hole by hole.
- Win a hole = 1 point. Most points at the end wins.
- Results are tracked on your **1v1 Rank** profile, separate from your main rank.

### 2v2 Scramble

- Two teams of two players each.
- Both teammates hit; the team plays from the best shot (scramble format).
- Results tracked on your **2v2 Scramble Rank** profile.

> **Tip:** You can play Fun or Competitive without activating a challenge format. Challenges are optional.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does my game mode choice affect the course I book at?**
No — you can pick any mode at any course.

**Can we change game modes mid-round?**
No. The mode is set before the round begins and cannot be changed once scores are being submitted.

**Do all players in the party have to agree on the mode?**
Yes. The party votes and all players must confirm the selected mode before the round starts.

**If I play 1v1 every round, does that replace my main rank?**
No. Your 1v1 Rank and 2v2 Scramble Rank are tracked **separately** from your main rank. All three can grow independently.
