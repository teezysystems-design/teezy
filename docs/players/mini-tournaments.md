# Mini Tournaments

## What Is a Mini Tournament?

Mini tournaments are short competitive windows set up by golf courses. When a course runs one, any player who books a round during that window can enter and compete on a live leaderboard for that course.

They're a great way to add a layer of competition to your regular rounds — with zero extra setup on your end.

---

## How to Enter a Mini Tournament

1. When booking a round, check the course card — active mini tournaments are highlighted.
2. When you start your round, you'll see an **Opt In** button if a mini tournament is active.
3. Tap **Opt In** before your round begins.
4. Play your round, log scores hole by hole.
5. Submit your scores at the end. The leaderboard updates in real time.

> **Important:** You must have **at least one playing partner** for your score to count in a mini tournament. Solo rounds are not eligible (integrity rule).

---

## Leaderboard

- View the live leaderboard during and after the tournament window from the course page or the Tournaments tab.
- At the end of the tournament, the winner is announced and results are posted to the social feed.

---

## How Scoring Works

Mini tournaments use **stroke play** format — lowest score wins. Scores are adjusted for course difficulty, consistent with the rest of the Teezy scoring system.

---

## Mini Tournament Stats on Your Profile

Your profile includes a dedicated **Mini Tournaments** section:

| Stat | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| Total entered | All tournaments you've participated in |
| Wins | Tournaments you've won |
| Podium finishes | Top 3 finishes |
| Courses played | Which courses you've competed at |

Tournament win badges are also displayed on your profile (e.g., "5x Tournament Champion").

---

## Do Mini Tournaments Affect My Main Rank?

**No.** Mini tournament results are completely separate from your main rank. Winning a tournament doesn't move your rank up or down — it's purely about the tournament leaderboard.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I enter more than one tournament at the same course?**
Yes, as long as each tournament has a separate active window and you book a round during that window.

**What if I forget to opt in before my round?**
You cannot opt in after the round starts. Make sure to tap Opt In before you tee off.

**Can I play a Chill round and still enter a mini tournament?**
No. To enter a mini tournament, you must play in Fun or Competitive mode and have at least one playing partner.

**What does the course offer as a prize?**
Prizes and promotions are entirely up to the course. Some offer discounts, pro shop credit, or recognition. Check the tournament details on the course page.

**Where can I see upcoming tournaments near me?**
The discovery feed highlights courses with active events and tournaments. You can also check the **Tournaments** tab directly.
