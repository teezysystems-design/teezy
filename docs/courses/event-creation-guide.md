# Event Creation Guide

## What Are Events & Promotions?

Events and promotions let you advertise what's happening at your course directly in the Teezy discovery feed and social feed. When you post an event, golfers nearby see it — even if they've never visited your course before. It's free marketing built into the platform.

Examples: Ladies Night, Couples Night, Happy Hour, Twilight Specials, Club Championship, New Member Drive, Food & Drink Specials.

---

## Step-by-Step: Create an Event

1. Log in to your **Manager Dashboard**.
2. Navigate to **Events & Promotions** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **Create Event**.
4. Fill in the event details:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Event name** | Yes | e.g., "Friday Happy Hour", "Ladies Night", "Junior Clinic" |
| **Date** | Yes | Date and time the event takes place |
| **Description** | Yes | What the event is, who it's for, any special details |
| **Image** | Optional | A photo or graphic to go with the post (strongly recommended) |

5. Click **Publish**. Your event will appear:
   - On your course's profile card in the discovery feed
   - In the Teezy social/events feed for nearby players
   - On the profiles of players who follow your course

## Managing Events

From the **Events & Promotions** section you can:

- View all upcoming and past events
- Edit an event (before the event date)
- Delete / cancel an event
- See how many players have viewed or expressed interest

---

## Tips for Great Events

- **Add an image.** Events with photos get significantly more views. Use a bright, on-brand photo.
- **Write a clear description.** Include the basics: what, when, who, and any special pricing or prizes.
- **Post early.** Give golfers at least a week's notice so they can plan a booking around your event.
- **Promote regularly.** A course that posts consistent events builds a following on Teezy, driving repeat bookings.

---

## Events vs. Mini Tournaments

| Feature | Events & Promotions | Mini Tournaments |
|---------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Purpose | Awareness & promotion | Competitive leaderboard |
| Players can opt in? | No action required — informational | Yes, explicit opt-in before round |
| Affects player rank? | No | No |
| Shows in discovery feed? | Yes | Yes |
| Sets up a leaderboard? | No | Yes |

You can run a tournament and an event at the same time — for example, post a "Spring Tournament" event while also having the tournament window active.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I post recurring events (e.g., every Friday)?**
Not automatically. You'll need to create each event individually. We're working on a recurring events feature for a future update.

**Can I link an event to a booking discount?**
Not directly through the event post, but you can mention discount codes or special pricing in the event description.

**How long does an event stay visible after it passes?**
Events are archived after the event date. They remain in your dashboard history but are removed from the active feed.

**Is there a limit to how many events I can post?**
No — post as many as you like. More events = more feed presence.
