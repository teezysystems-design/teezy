# Teezy Pricing for Courses

## Overview

Teezy is **100% free for golfers**. Courses pay a flat per-booking fee, invoiced monthly via Stripe. There are no setup fees, no monthly subscriptions, and no lock-in contracts.

---

## Standard Rate

**$2.75 per completed booking**

You're only charged for bookings that are completed — no charge for cancellations or no-shows.

---

## Early Adopter Discount Tiers

Courses that actively promote Teezy to their golfers unlock reduced rates. The more you promote, the less you pay per booking. Rates are locked in permanently once earned.

| What you do | Your rate |
|-------------|-----------|
| Add Teezy to your website or booking page | **$2.25/booking** |
| Actively push golfers to book through Teezy (signage, staff mentions) | **$2.00/booking** |
| Run a mini tournament through Teezy + promote on your socials | **$1.75/booking** |
| **Founding course** (among the first courses to sign up — locked in forever) | **$1.50/booking** |

> **Tiers are cumulative and permanent.** Once your course qualifies for a tier, your rate is locked in at that level and will never go up — even as Teezy grows.

---

## Billing & Invoicing

- Invoices are generated **monthly** based on completed bookings.
- Payment is processed via **Stripe**. You'll provide bank or card details when your course signs up.
- You can view all billing history and download invoices from your **Manager Dashboard**.

---

## How Golfers Pay

Teezy never handles golfer payments. Golfers pay your course directly — using whatever process you already have (at the pro shop, pre-paid online, etc.). Teezy simply connects you with the golfer and handles the booking.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**When am I charged — at booking or at play?**
You're charged per **completed booking**, meaning the golfer showed up and played. Cancellations are not billed.

**How do I qualify for the $2.25 tier?**
Add a link to Teezy on your website or booking page and notify your Teezy account manager. Once verified, your rate adjusts on the next invoice.

**What counts as "actively pushing golfers to book through Teezy"?**
Physical signage at your course (e.g., banners or counter cards) and staff actively mentioning Teezy when golfers call in or walk up to book. Reach out to your account manager to verify and claim this tier.

**Is the founding course rate still available?**
The founding course rate ($1.50/booking) is only available to early adopters — spots are limited. Contact us to find out if it's still available.

**What happens if I stop promoting Teezy after locking in a discount tier?**
Your rate is locked in permanently regardless of your ongoing promotion level. We honor the commitment you made.

**Is there a minimum booking volume?**
No minimum. You pay only for what you use.
