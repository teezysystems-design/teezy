# Tournament Creation Guide

## What Is a Mini Tournament?

A mini tournament is a short competitive window you set up at your course. During the window, golfers who book a round can opt in and compete on a live leaderboard. It's free for golfers, drives more bookings for you, and gets promoted in the Teezy discovery feed automatically.

---

## Step-by-Step: Create a Mini Tournament

1. Log in to your **Manager Dashboard**.
2. Navigate to **Tournaments** in the left sidebar.
3. Click **Create Tournament**.
4. Fill in the tournament details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Tournament name** | A catchy name (e.g., "Friday Showdown", "Spring Classic") |
| **Start date** | When the tournament window opens |
| **End date** | When the tournament window closes (can be daily or weekly) |
| **Format** | Stroke play (standard) |
| **Prize / Promo** *(optional)* | Describe the reward — pro shop credit, discount, bragging rights |

5. Click **Publish**. The tournament is now live and will appear in:
   - Your course's discovery card
   - The Teezy events feed
   - The player's tournament opt-in flow when they book a round at your course

---

## How Players Enter

Players do not need to do anything special to find your tournament — it surfaces automatically during the booking flow. Before they start their round, they'll see an **Opt In** button. Scoring and leaderboard updates happen automatically once they submit their round scores.

> **Integrity rule:** Players must have at least one playing partner for their score to count. Solo rounds are excluded from mini tournaments.

---

## Managing an Active Tournament

From the **Tournaments** section in your Manager Dashboard, you can:

- View the live leaderboard at any time
- See which players have opted in
- Edit tournament details (while no scores have been submitted)
- End the tournament early if needed

---

## After the Tournament

When the tournament window closes:

1. The leaderboard is finalized.
2. The winner and results are automatically posted to the Teezy social feed.
3. Tournament results appear on each participating player's profile.
4. You can announce the winner and award any prizes directly.

---

## Tips for Running a Great Tournament

- **Set a clear prize.** Even a small pro shop credit drives significantly more opt-ins.
- **Promote it.** Share the tournament on your course's social channels. Players can share tournament links too.
- **Run weekend tournaments.** Friday–Sunday windows capture the most traffic.
- **Running a tournament and promoting it on your socials** qualifies you for the **$1.75/booking** pricing tier (see [Pricing Model](./pricing-model.md)).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I run multiple tournaments at the same time?**
No. Only one active tournament per course at a time to keep the leaderboard clear and competitive.

**Can I make a tournament invite-only?**
No — mini tournaments are open to any player who books a round during the window.

**What scoring format is used?**
Stroke play (lowest total score wins). Scores are adjusted for course difficulty, consistent with Teezy's ranking system.

**Do tournament results affect players' main rank?**
No. Mini tournament results are completely separate from the main ranking system.

**Is there a minimum number of players for a valid tournament?**
There is no minimum. If only 2 players opt in, a winner is still declared. Promoting your tournament will drive more participants.
