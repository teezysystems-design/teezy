# Competitive Intelligence — PAR-Tee

Strategic analysis of the golf app landscape and PAR-Tee's competitive position.

## Competitor Landscape

| App | Strength | Weakness | PAR-Tee Advantage |
|-----|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **GolfNow** | Largest tee time inventory, established brand | No social/ranking layer, UX dated | Social + competitive layer on top of booking |
| **The Grint** | Handicap tracking, active community | Booking is secondary, not GPS-focused | Mood-based discovery, AI matching |
| **Arccos Caddie** | AI shot tracking, sensor hardware | Requires expensive sensors, niche | No hardware required, casual-friendly |
| **18Birdies** | Scorecard + GPS, clean UX | Weak social, no ranking gamification | Rank tiers, leagues, real social feed |
| **GolfShot** | GPS accuracy, course maps | Premium paywall heavy, aging design | Free social layer, no GPS paywalls |
| **Hole19** | European market strong, GPS | Limited US inventory, no AI features | US-focused, Claude mood matching |

## PAR-Tee Differentiators

1. **Mood-based discovery** — "Tell me what kind of round you want" (Claude AI)
2. **Gamified ranking system** — Tier ladder (Rookie → Legend) with points per round
3. **Social feed with competitive context** — Round scores, rank-ups, league results in one feed
4. **1v1 and 2v2 leagues** — Commissioner-driven, invite-only seasons with brackets
5. **Course-created tournaments** — Courses drive engagement, golfers opt in during booking
6. **Party system** — Invite friends after booking, see who's available

## Pricing Analysis

| App | Model | Price |
|-----|-------|-------|
| GolfNow | Commission on bookings + premium | Free + booking fees |
| The Grint | Freemium | Free / $9.99/mo premium |
| Arccos | Hardware + subscription | $179 sensors + $99/yr |
| 18Birdies | Freemium | Free / $6/mo premium |

**PAR-Tee opportunity**: Free core + league/tournament entry fees (Stripe Connect) + course booking commission
