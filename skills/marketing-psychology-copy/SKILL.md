# Marketing Psychology and Copy — PAR-Tee

Conversion-focused copy and growth strategy for the PAR-Tee golfer app.

## Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Competitive but welcoming. Like a good golf buddy — encouraging, slightly irreverent, never stuffy.
- **Personality**: Knows golf well, loves the game, wants you to play more and play better.
- **Avoid**: Country club elitism, technical jargon without explanation, overselling.
- **Core promise**: "Find your game. Play your way."

## Target Audience

| Segment | Profile | Motivator |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Social Golfer | 25–40, plays 1–2x/month, loves the hang | Friends, easy booking, no commitment |
| Competitive Amateur | 30–50, 8–18 handicap, tracks stats | Rankings, leagues, beating their buddies |
| Golf Explorer | 20–35, new to golf or new to an area | Discovery, mood matching, no intimidation |
| Course Regular | Any age, loyal to 1–3 courses | Rewards, familiar faces, community |

## Push Notification Copy

Max 2/day/user. Each must earn its send.

```
# Rank-up
🏆 You hit [Tier]! [X] more points to [Next Tier].

# Party invite
⛳ [Friend] is teeing off Saturday — you in?

# Tournament starting
🎖️ [Tournament Name] starts today. Check the leaderboard.

# Weekly availability
📅 New week — update your availability so friends can find you.
```

**Rules**: Use name when available. Never send two notifications of the same type on the same day.

## App Store Listing

### Title
PAR-Tee: Golf, Ranked & Social

### Subtitle
Book Tees. Compete. Connect.

### Description (Short)
Find courses that match your mood. Book tee times. Compete in leagues and mini-tournaments. Track your rank as you level up from Rookie to Legend.

### Keywords
golf booking, golf app, golf tracker, tee time, golf league, golf scorecard, golf handicap, golf social, golf near me

## Onboarding Copy

```
Screen 1: "What kind of golfer are you?"
→ Mood tags: Competitive, Relaxed, Social, Scenic, Beginner-friendly

Screen 2: "Your first round is on us"
→ Discover courses → Book in 3 taps

Screen 3: "Join the ranks"
→ Every round earns points. Every point builds your legacy.
```

## Growth Levers

1. **Viral loop**: Post-round share card → "Play PAR-Tee" CTA
2. **League hook**: Commissioner invites friends → each needs the app
3. **Course partnership**: Courses post tournament → golfers download to register
4. **AI word-of-mouth**: "Tell me what kind of round you want" is genuinely novel — demo video hook
